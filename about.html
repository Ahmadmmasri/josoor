Our Origin

Simplifying Your Medical Journey With Josoor care.

We are a team of Arabic-speaking medical doctors based in the UK, committed to connecting patients with world-class medical care. Understanding the challenges of accessing top specialists—along with the language and administrative barriers that can complicate the process—we provide seamless support every step of the way.

While medical decisions remain with the experts, our role is to ensure a smooth and stress-free experience. From translation services and direct communication with doctors to coordinating consultations and arranging comfortable accommodations through our trusted partners, we make medical travel effortless.
